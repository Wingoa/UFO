UFO Project for Digital Humanities Fall 2022

This project was created to collect, organize, and summarize a small achive of UFO reports release by the United Kingdom governemnt in 2013. These documents include testimonials and reports from a wide range of professional law enforcement/military personnel and civilians who had personnally witnessed an unidentifed object.

All source materials were collected from the UFOs section of the National Archives website. All content was available under the
Open Government Licence v3.0.
